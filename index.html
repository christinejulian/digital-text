Echoes of the Titanic

April 14, 1912. The RMS Titanic sails across a calm Atlantic Ocean beneath a sky overflowing with stars. Passengers admire the elegance of the world's newest luxury liner while enjoying music, fine dining, and lively conversation. No one imagines history is only hours away.

You stand beneath the magnificent Grand Staircase. Its polished oak, glittering chandelier, and sweeping staircase make it feel like a floating palace. Nearby, children laugh while wealthy travelers discuss business opportunities awaiting them in New York.

Suddenly... a faint vibration travels through the floor. The engines slow. Silence spreads through the ship.

Historical Fact:
Titanic struck an iceberg at approximately 11:40 PM on April 14, 1912.

Crew members begin uncovering lifeboats. Some passengers laugh, refusing to believe anything could threaten the world's largest ship. Others grow uneasy as the ship lists ever so slightly.

In the wireless room, frantic operators send distress signals into the night: "CQD... CQD... SOS..."

Down in the engine room, water pours into the lower compartments. The truth becomes undeniable: the Titanic is dying.

Lifeboats lower into the freezing Atlantic. The orchestra plays its final hymn. The bow sinks deeper, pulling the great ship toward its fate.

Hours later, survivors drift among stars and ice. A distant glow appears on the horizon—the RMS Carpathia, answering the call of distress.

Ending I — Safe Escape

You reach a lifeboat in time. The Carpathia rescues you at dawn, its lights shimmering across the icy sea.

Ending II — Too Late

You return to the deck too late. Every lifeboat is gone. The ship tilts sharply as the Atlantic claims it.

Ending III — The Last Lifeboat

A crew member guides you through a hidden passage. You escape in one of the final lifeboats, drifting under a sky full of stars.

Ending IV — Waiting Too Long

You hesitate, believing the danger will pass. When you finally act, the ship is already beyond saving.

Ending V — The Echo Below

You follow a strange noise into the lower decks. Shadows move. The whisper of the ship’s final breath becomes your last memory.
