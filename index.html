Echoes of the Titanic

April 14, 1912. The RMS Titanic moves confidently across a calm Atlantic beneath a sky filled with brilliant stars. Inside the ship, music echoes through elegant halls while passengers enjoy dinner, conversation, and dreams of arriving in New York. Crew members perform their duties with quiet confidence because the ship is celebrated as a masterpiece of engineering. You pause beside the Grand Staircase, admiring polished wood, glowing lights, and the excitement surrounding the voyage. Suddenly, a gentle vibration passes beneath your feet. It lasts only a moment, yet whispers spread among nearby passengers. Some dismiss it as a routine movement, while others glance toward the windows with growing concern. You must decide what to do next.

The Open Deck

Cold air greets you as you step outside. The ocean is unusually still, making the silence feel unsettling. An officer quickly walks toward the bridge while several sailors stare into the darkness ahead. A few passengers laugh and continue their evening, convinced nothing could threaten such a magnificent ship.

Follow the Officer Ignore the Warning

The Grand Interior

The orchestra resumes playing, but the music feels different now. A steward politely asks guests to wear life jackets. Some obey immediately while others smile and refuse, believing there is no danger. You hesitate, wondering whether to trust the warning or return to your cabin.

Ending One — Safe Escape

You follow the crew without hesitation. Your quick decision allows you to board a lifeboat before panic spreads across the decks. From the freezing Atlantic you watch the Titanic slowly disappear beneath the waves. Rescue eventually arrives, and although the memory of that night never leaves you, your courage and willingness to act gave you a second chance.

Ending Two — Lost to History

Believing the ship is unsinkable, you remain on deck until chaos erupts. Lifeboats are gone before you realize the truth. The lights fade, the bow slips beneath the water, and the Atlantic becomes silent once again. Your story joins the countless lives remembered from one of history's greatest maritime tragedies.

Ending Three — Fortune Smiles

While collecting your belongings, you discover a narrow service corridor where a crew member leads a handful of passengers to one of the final lifeboats.
