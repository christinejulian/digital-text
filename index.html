Echoes of the Titanic

April 14, 1912. The RMS Titanic sails across a calm Atlantic Ocean beneath a sky overflowing with stars. Passengers admire the elegance of the world's newest luxury liner while enjoying music, fine dining, and lively conversation. No one imagines history is only hours away.

You stand beneath the magnificent Grand Staircase. Its polished oak, glittering chandelier, and sweeping staircase make it feel like a floating palace. Nearby, children laugh while wealthy travelers discuss business opportunities awaiting them in New York.

Suddenly... a faint vibration travels through the floor. It is gentle enough that many people barely notice. Others pause, exchanging uncertain looks. The engines slow. Silence spreads through the ship.

Historical Fact:
Titanic struck an iceberg at approximately 11:40 PM on April 14, 1912.
