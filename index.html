Ending One - Survivor

You stop an officer and ask what happened. He calmly tells you to put on a life jacket and proceed to the lifeboats immediately. Because you acted quickly, you board one of the first boats. Hours later you watch from a distance as the Titanic disappears beneath the Atlantic. You survive and spend the rest of your life remembering that one decision saved you.

Ending Two - Too Late
